About Me

My name is Saida Nachit. I am from Morocco, specifically city of Casablanca. On the 25th of August, 2013, my family and I moved to live in the United Arab Emirates. It was tough in the beginning leaving our family and Morocco behind but eventually, we got used to it and adapted to live in the UAE. That experience taught me many valuable lessons; it taught me to respect and live harmoniously with different cultures and traditions.

I did Media at university in another country when I was younger, and it was way too theory-based learning. Most of the learning was done through readings, and there were very few times to practice what we learned in reality. I wanted more from that experience, more hands-on learning, more creative exploration, and more real-world application. That is why I chose IMD.
